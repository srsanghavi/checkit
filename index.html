What is OMR? How is it Important?

OMRs’ are a method of converting the data obtained from humans into data that can be read by the computers. Optical Mark Readers consider marks made by humans as answers to a set of questions. OMRS are crucial in today’s age of testing and competition. Most of the renowned national and international competitive exams set multiple options papers where OMRs play a crucial role. OMRs remove ambiguity from the testing process. It is crucial for children to learn to be familiar with OMR sheets.

What is the traditional way of checking OMR sheets? Why is it not a good idea to check manually?

Traditionally OMR sheets are checked using the expensive scanners and softwares available in the market. The problem with them is that they are extremely expensive that is they literally burn a hole in your pocket. Thus, the organizations that are unable to afford this cost choose to check the sheets manually. The main purpose of OMR sheets is to decrease human effort. But on the contrary the manual checking process increases the effort exponentially. The process of actually carefully going through sets of circular marks is an even more uphill task then checking regular answer sheets.
